About us

At Airbnb, we believe in the transformative power of travel and its ability to connect people from all walks of life. Founded in 2008, Airbnb has become a global community marketplace that offers unique stays and experiences around the world. Our platform is built on the idea that every person should be able to belong anywhere. Whether you're a traveler seeking authentic experiences or a host looking to share your space and passions, Airbnb provides a platform that fosters connections and creates memorable moments.
